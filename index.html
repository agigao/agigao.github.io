      • And category - from the one before it: 2
      • The result: subcategory_name - Gabriel Iglesias, category: Comedy
        Exactly the way it's mapped in our yey_subcategory table.
      • Then during subcategory extraction - we compare subcategory_names for

    Well, this might not be the silver bullet to our problem, but we can grab data and do the analysis regarding the subcategories that we have in db.
